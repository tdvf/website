Mudhewadi Development Overview

Motivation towards development of Mudhewadi village
  • Cities are getting crowded these days and village peoples are unwillingly moving towards city due to unemployment in villages. Hence to stop migration we need to develop the village in all perspectives.
  • Our aim is to develop a smart village which can provide all basic needs to the people including Health care, bank & post office facility, employment, latest technologies
  • To Develop a model village which can be a motivation factor for other nearby villages to start thinking towards development.
  • To Develop a ideal village which can be a unique in its own perspective like landscaping, modern technologies.
  • To develop a green village by utilizing nature friendly sources like bio-gas plant, rain water harvesting, solar lights , water shed management etc.
